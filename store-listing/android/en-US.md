## Full description
CryptoPortfolio is a professional mobile crypto tracker built for investors who want clarity, speed, and control.

Track your portfolio with live market pricing, clean analytics, and practical transaction tools designed for daily use.

Key features:
- Live portfolio value with per-asset breakdown
- Cost basis, realized gains, and total gains tracking
- Fast chart ranges (1H, 1D, 1W, 1M, 1Y, ALL)
- CSV import/export for transaction history
- Multi-currency support with localized formatting
- Local-first data persistence for reliability
- Lightweight interface optimized for quick checks

Designed for active monitoring:
- See your portfolio trend at a glance
- Review coin-level performance and transaction context
- Refresh market data on demand
- Keep a structured record of buys, sells, and transfers

CryptoPortfolio focuses on practical portfolio tracking without unnecessary complexity.

Disclaimer:
CryptoPortfolio provides portfolio analytics and market information only. It does not provide financial advice.
